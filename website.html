Indian Cricket Team represents India in international cricket under BCCI. India has produced legendary players admired across the world. The team performs strongly in Test, ODI and T20 formats. Popularly known as Men in Blue.
India has won ICC Cricket World Cup, T20 World Cup and Champions Trophy. The team is known for discipline and fighting spirit.
Legends like Sachin Tendulkar, MS Dhoni, Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma built India's cricket legacy.
